BreadHaus

Project

BreadHaus is an artisan bread brand born in New York City, blending modern design with the soul of the city.

Scope

Bag design, logo, branding, poster promotion, flyers

Year

2025

Project summary

BreadHaus is an artisan bread brand born in New York City, blending modern design with the soul of the city. The identity plays off the contrast between old-world craft and urban energy. The logo is a custom bread tag, symbolizing everyday familiarity reimagined. It’s iconic, minimal, and instantly recognizable—evoking freshness, utility, and nostalgia all at once.

There are 4 locations: Soho, East Village, Bushwick, and Brooklyn. I've created a bread tag design for each, naming the crossroads on the top half. When you make a purchase, you will receive a bag to carry your bread which has been thoughtfully designed. Hanging from one of the handles is a location-specific tag—a tactile detail that connects each loaf to the neighborhood it came from, making every visit feel personal and place-based.

The main color palette pairs are chartreuse and chocolate brown. The secondary colors are lighter and muted tones of each. Chartreuse adds a jolt of vibrancy, reflecting NYC’s constant motion and edge. Chocolate brown grounds the brand, echoing the warmth of a crisp crust and neighborhood bakeries.

I was originally assigned to design a carefully crafted paper bag featuring a custom logo. From that starting point, I developed BreadHaus. Pushing beyond the brief, I expanded the identity system to include eight unique posters and flyers, designed for promotion on the streets of NYC.
